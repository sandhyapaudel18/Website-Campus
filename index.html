COURSES WE OFFER

We offer diverse array of courses offered at our Campus, including BCA, BBS and B.Ed, tailored to empower you for a successful future.

01.

BCA

In the Bachelor of Computer Application(BCA) program covers essential computer science topics like programming, databases, and web technologies. Through hands-on projects and internships, students gain practical skills and industry experience.

02.

BBS

In the Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS) program, students explore key concepts in business administration and management. From marketing strategies to financial analysis, the curriculum provides a comprehensive understanding of the business world.

03.

B.ED

In the Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) program, students delve into the theory and practice of teaching and learning. From educational psychology to pedagogical techniques, the curriculum offers a holistic understanding of the field of education.
